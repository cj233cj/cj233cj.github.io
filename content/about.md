This is a personal blog where I put random thoughts that feel too long for a chat message and too unfinished for anything serious.
 
Topics drift around — games, anime, manga, novels, politics and whatever's been sitting in my head lately. Sometimes a quick impression, sometimes a *critical* rant.
 
The site is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io) and a minimal theme ["memo"](https://github.com/cj233cj/cj233cj.github.io/tree/master/themes/memo) I vibe-coded with Claude (*Free Tier* btw). It looks clean and works fine.
 
The author is not a native English speaker, so don't expect fascinating words. And for one certain reason, the author does not speak Chinese here — unfortunately.

The Hugo theme "memo" is MIT licensed, but the contents are not — please don't mess around without consent.
