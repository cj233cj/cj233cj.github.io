- Destiny 2 has been a victim of its own success, which wasn't that big anyway.
- Bungie wants to make an MMO FPS, but right now Destiny 2 is an MMO build simulator basically: grinding and hoarding perks on guns is not fun because skill mechanics are overwhelmingly complex... and behold, the 7 layers of Destiny 2 perk system:
    - weapon types, perks, elements
    - armor mods, perks, base stats
    - exotic weapon and exotic armor perks
    - super skill with grenade, melee, classical skill and aspects
    - seasonal artifact perks
    - seasonal vendor modifiers and consumable buffs
    - season activity ingame perks  
    …this is simply preposterous.
