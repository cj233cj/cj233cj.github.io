'Make Heroine ga Oosugiru! ' is one of the most delicate anime series i've watched, the bright clean color design together with the art style blends with both manga and gouache aesthetics is brilliant, the theme music for 3 main characters is also touching and catchy;

but i prefer manga version more because manga has more context details and a more humorous art style, more fun moments actually, anime is still kinda formalized.
